How to Play

Generate or write a riddle, encrypt it, then solve the answer. Harder difficulties, faster answers, fewer hints, and stronger glitch intensity give better scores.

  • Decode Lens strips visual glitch markers and reveals a cleaned signal preview.
  • Adaptive difficulty follows your streak: it gets harder as you improve.
  • Fuzzy Answer Check accepts small typos, aliases, and article-free answers.
  • Everything is local: progress, custom riddles, and leaderboard stay in your browser storage.
